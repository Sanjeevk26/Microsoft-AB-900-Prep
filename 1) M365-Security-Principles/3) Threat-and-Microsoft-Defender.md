# Threats & Microsoft Defender XDR

## Overview

In today’s digital world, there are numerous threats to data and systems. Organizations must understand these threats and implement strong protection mechanisms using intelligent security solutions like **Microsoft Defender XDR (Extended Detection and Response)**.

---

## Common Cyber Threats

### 1. Malware
- Short for **malicious software**
- Can:
  - Steal sensitive information  
  - Lock systems (ransomware)  
  - Use systems to send spam  
- Often installed unknowingly by users  

---

### 2. Data Breach
A data breach occurs when unauthorized access is gained to sensitive data.

**Causes include:**
- Unauthorized third-party access  
- Human error (sending data to wrong person)  
- Lost or stolen devices  
- Unauthorized data modification  
- Data unavailability  

> Example: In the UK, breaches must be reported within **72 hours** to the ICO.

---

### 3. Dictionary Attack / Password Spraying
- Attempts common passwords across many accounts  
- Exploits weak passwords (e.g., `password123`)  
- Also known as **password spraying**

---

### 4. Ransomware
- Locks users out of systems or data  
- Requires payment (ransom) for access  
- Access may not be restored even after payment  

---

### 5. Phishing
- Tricks users into sharing sensitive information  
- Often via fake emails or websites  
- “Phishing” refers to **baiting users like fishing**

---

### 6. DDoS (Distributed Denial of Service)
- Overloads systems or websites with traffic  
- Causes service disruption or downtime  

---

Threat protection helps defend systems against cyber threats using:

## Microsoft Defender XDR

Microsoft Defender XDR (formerly **Microsoft 365 Defender**) is a unified security solution.

### Key Capabilities:
- Preventative protection  
- Post-breach detection  
- Automated investigation & response  
- Threat hunting across systems  

---

## Defender XDR Components

### 1. Defender for Endpoint
- Protects devices (endpoints)  
- Detects and responds to threats  

---

### 2. Defender Vulnerability Management
- Provides:
  - Asset visibility  
  - Risk-based assessments  
  - Remediation tools  

---

### 3. Defender for Office 365
- Protects:
  - Email systems  
  - Collaboration tools  
- Detects malicious links and attachments  

---

### 4. Defender for Identity
- Protects against identity-based threats  
- Works with **Microsoft Entra ID Protection**  

---

### 5. Defender for Cloud Apps
- Secures cloud applications  
- Provides:
  - Data control  
  - Threat detection  

---

## Key Features of Defender XDR

- Signal sharing across services  
- Unified security events view  
- Automated threat response  
- Cross-domain threat hunting (endpoints, identities, data)
